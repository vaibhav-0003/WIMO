About this Page !

Being a FOODIE, I constantly order food from ZOMATO. At times I get confused, “What should I order ?” and it gets quite frustrating when you’re unable to choose what to watch on Netflix while eating and on top of that you can’t even choose what to EAT!!!

So as I was learning Web Development, I had an idea why shouldn’t I make something which will give me some random suggestions from my favorite set of dishes and I’ll Zomato it.

So here it is XD. I don’t know how useful it will be for others but it was so much fun making it!!
